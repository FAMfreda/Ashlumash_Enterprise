About Our Products

Sustainable Art from Rural Ghana

At Ashlumash Enterprise, we transform discarded water bottles, rubber, polythene, and plastic spoons into beautiful wall decorations and flowerpots.

Each piece is carefully crafted by hand in our workshop in Asunafo North, Ahafo Region, bringing new life to materials that would otherwise pollute our environment.

Meet the Innovator

From Challenge to Opportunity

Ashlumash Enterprise was founded by a determined woman who didn't let physical challenges stop her from making a difference. Born and raised in rural Ahafo Region, she saw the growing problem of plastic waste in her community and decided to take action.

Through creativity and perseverance, she developed techniques to transform waste materials into beautiful decorative items, creating both environmental impact and economic opportunity in her rural community.

"Every discarded bottle has potential. I see beauty where others see trash."
